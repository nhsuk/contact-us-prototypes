I would like to access my own or another persons medical records

The NHS website team can help with questions and feedback about the website. But we can’t provide personal medical information such as access to your health records.

How do I access my health records?

If you’ve had a medical or health appointment, or any treatment, then you’ll have a health record.

To access your health records you need to contact the service that you visited.

This could be a:

  • GP practice
  • hospital
  • optician
  • dentist

It’s free for you to access your health records, and healthcare staff must give you access to your health records if you ask for them.

See more information on how to access your health records

GP online services

You can also request GP records online using GP online services.

Find out more about GP online services

If you want to access someone else’s records

Health and care records are confidential. You can only access someone else’s records if you’ve been given permission to do so.

Find out more about accessing someone else’s medical records
